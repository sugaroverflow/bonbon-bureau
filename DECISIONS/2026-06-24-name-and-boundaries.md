# 2026-06-24: Name and boundaries

## Status

Accepted

## Context

The system needs names that are cute, memorable, and useful for explaining the architecture publicly.

## Decision

- The named-agent server/setup is **Bonbon Bureau**.
- The future local factory/hive is **Candyland**.
- A future connector may be called **Jellybean Junction**.

## Consequences

Bonbon Bureau can be discussed publicly as the agent/control-plane layer without implying it owns local utility-worker automation.
