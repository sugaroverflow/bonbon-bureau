# 2026-06-24: Miette and Idea Pantry

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Bonbon Bureau needs names that are cute, memorable, and still useful for explaining the architecture publicly.

## Decision

- The Operator/control-plane agent is named **Miette**.
- The future-ideas parking lot is named **Idea Pantry**.

## Consequences

Miette gives the Operator role a named-agent identity while preserving the role's clear responsibilities: routing, status, blockers, and escalation.

Idea Pantry keeps maybe-later ideas visible without letting them distract from the immediate Bureau scaffold.
